Black Gulaman Drink: Simply Refreshing

Black gulaman is a popular Filipino drink made from black jelly cubes combined in a brown sugar syrup called arnibal. It is often served chilled with ice and is commonly enjoyed as a refreshing beverage, especially during hot weather. The drink is typically paired with snacks like fishballs and is a staple at street food stalls in the Philippines.

The key to a delicious Black Gulaman is the right amount of sweet and a whole lotta love.

- Kenn Gob, Food Lover

Ingredients:

  • Grass Jelly: 1 Small Can
  • Sugar: 1/2 cup
  • Vanilla Extract: A few drops
  • Water: 1 Liter

Preparation:

  1. Slice grass jelly into small cubes.
  2. Make sure that the pitcher is filled with water only about 70% of the way.
  3. Mix in the grass jelly cubes and stir well.
  4. Add white sugar to taste. For 1 liter, 1/2 cup of sugar is preferred.
  5. Add in 3-4 drops of vanilla essence/extract and stir well once again.
  6. Chill for about 2 hours or serve with ice. Enjoy!

Cooking Tips:

  • Use banana essence instead of vanilla for a different, exciting flavor.
